    Greetings! I’m Vedant Anand, a passionate Mechatronics (with specialization in Robotics) Engineer with a strong background in design and manufacturing. Over the past few years, I’ve had the privilege of working on a diverse range of projects, including leading the design and fabrication of an Autonomous Underwater Vehicle (AUV) Team at SRM University, where I utilized tools such as SolidWorks, Fusion360 to create and deploy mechanical systems capable of real-time navigation. My experiences also include working on advanced mechanism integration for robotic arms used in culinary and hospitality, and developing mobility systems for autonomous rovers designed for extraterrestrial exploration. Moving forward, I’m excited to dive deeper into projects involving AI-driven robotics and intelligent automation systems, particularly in the fields of race engineering. I’m eager to collaborate on cutting-edge projects that challenge conventional limits and leverage my expertise in robotics, electro-mechanical systems, and design to bring innovative solutions to life.
